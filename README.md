# 🇿🇦 MzansiEd — Your AI CAPS Study Companion

**MzansiEd** is a cutting-edge, AI-powered educational platform designed specifically for South African students following the **CAPS curriculum**. Built by passionate Computer Science students, MzansiEd aims to democratize access to high-quality tutoring and help learners excel in every subject with confidence.

🌐 **Visit us at:** [https://mzansied.co.za](https://mzansied.co.za)

## ✨ Core Features

- **Ask & Understand**: Instant AI-driven chat for clear, grade-appropriate explanations and help with complex concepts.
- **Solve Homework**: Guidance on tough problems through scanning or typing questions, providing step-by-step logic rather than just answers.
- **Practice Past Papers**: Integrated database of real South African past papers to master exam techniques and marking criteria.
- **Tailored Quizzes**: Dynamic test generation based on your weak spots and current grade level to track improvement.
- **Curated Video Lessons**: Targeted educational content from YouTube, selected to match CAPS topics.

## 📝 Mission

Our mission is to bridge the educational gap in South Africa. By combining elite-level AI logic with the specific needs of the local curriculum, we provide every student with a personal, intelligent tutor available 24/7.
